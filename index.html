How do I play?


There are four recycle bins below, each with a different random number assigned to it, click on them to add to your points.
The goal is to match the number exactly, giving yourself a win. If you go over you will be given a loss.
A new random number will be given once the game is over. This will also reset the value of each bin. Good luck and have fun.
